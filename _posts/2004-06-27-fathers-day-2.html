You sent me out.
I resisted.
You made me go.
I went.
Brian showed up
with LeAnne.
I broke his toe,
played Golden Tee.
(I lost)
You trusted me.
I missed you.
I saw a room.
You weren't there.
I drank
without you,
alone
with friends.
You sent me out.
I went.
I paid and left.
I walked.
You made me go.
I'm home now.
Father's day:
It's when a man
understands
that the woman
makes the man.
You made me.
I resisted.
I was wrong.
You made me.
I resisted.
I'm the man.
You made me.
