This Sunday, God willing, my family will attend a Tridentine High Mass in this beautiful church.

On our knees, the priest facing Christ crucified, Latin and chants invoking the Holy Spirit to fill our souls with graces. Does it get any better on this earth?

Gloria in excelsis Deo. Et in terra pax hominibus bonae voluntatis. Laudamus te. Benedicimus te. Adoramus te. Glorificamus te. Gratiam agimus tibi propter magnam gloriam tuam. Domine Deus, Rex coelestis, Deus Pater omnipotens. Domine Fili unigenite, Jesu Christe.
