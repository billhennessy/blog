You might have noticed that I'm linking to Jay Anderson's Pro Ecclesia quite a bit lately. You might think I'm selfishly skimming off his readers with cheap trackbacks, but that's not the case at all. The fact is, Jay provides the best round-up of news interesting to Catholics and Christians I've found. He provides full stories, great links, and pithy, on-the-nose comments.

If you're looking for great information on the state of God's world and its seeming indifference to Him, you'll find it at Pro Ecclesia.
