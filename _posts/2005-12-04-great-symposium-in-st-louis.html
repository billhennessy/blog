Canons Regular of the New Jerusalem will hold a three-day symposium centered around the Feast of the Immaculate Conception of the Blessed Virgin Mary and the Feast of Our Lady of Guadalupe on December 8, 9, and 10 at the Cardinal Rigali Center in Shrewsbury, MO (Map of 20 Archbishop May Dr Saint Louis, MO 63119-5738, US).

Please, please, please, if you live near St. Louis, plan on attending at least the Mass for the Immaculate Conception (a Holy Day of Obligation for all Latin Rite Catholics) on Thursday at 7:00 PM in the Saint Vincent de Paul Chapel at the Rigali Center. The celebrant and homilist will be Dom Daniel Augustine Oppenheimer, CRNJ. As always, this Mass will be celebrated according to the Traditional Latin Missale Romanum of 1962. Music for the Liturgy: William Byrd's Mass for Four Voices.

The rest of the symposium breaks down as follows:

Friday December 9, 2005
6:45 PM Registration ($10 payable at the door or by advance purchase)
7:00 PM The Apparitions of Our Lady of Guadalupe Dr. Lawrence Feingold
8:15 PM Our Lady at the Wedding Feast of Cana Dr. Kenneth Howell
Saturday December 10, 2005
8:00 AM Votive Mass of Our Lady of Guadalupe (traditional Latin Missale Romanum of 1962) Dr. Lawrence Feingold
9:00 AM Registration ($15 at the door or in advance)
9:15 AM Our Lady of Guadalupe brings Christ to the Americas Dr. Lawrence Feingold
10:30 AM Our Lady at the Foot of the Cross Dr. Kenneth Howell
11:45 AM "Am I Not Your Fountain of Life?" Dr. Lawrence Feingold

Advance registration & information call (636) 536-3229 or e-mail lawrencefeingold@sbcglobal.net.
