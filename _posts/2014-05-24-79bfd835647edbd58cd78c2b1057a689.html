Dear Mr. Hennessy,
I have been reading your stuff for a while. I am an old Tea Party guy. I gave the Jefferson County Tea party office space in one of my buildings when Ken Horton was in charge. They still use the space.
Now we rarely meet. The steam is gone. We were a mighty little group for a while. What happened?
When we meet everybody is at least 60. Where are the young people?
The bad guys come at us from so many directions at once it tires the soul.
Saul Alinsky was right, and Obama is following his message perfectly. What a shame that it is working so well.
Do have any thoughts on how to re start a group like ours?
We might as well not meet. nothing happens.
I will continue reading you.
Regards,
John W Wightman
