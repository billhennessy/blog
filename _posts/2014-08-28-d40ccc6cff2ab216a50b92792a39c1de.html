Dear Mr Hennessy

I am an Independent and claim no political party although I always vote. I had to write and tell you that I think the "Shop Ferguson" idea is excellent and I applaud you for that. Just wanted you to know that my husband and I emailed our State Rep. Tim Jones in support of the Tea Party's suggestion for a Tax Holiday for Ferguson.

I may not agree with everything you say, but you are thoughtful, make some good points, and present a civil, reasoned argument. The same things I liked about John Danforth. So you may not have a convert to the Tea Party, but you have a new reader.

Sincerely

Marie Vogler
