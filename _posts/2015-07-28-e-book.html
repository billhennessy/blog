I am a better person because I served.

The US Navy did far more for me than I did for the Navy.

I was a better person the day I left active duty than I am today. But I’m better today than I would have been had I not served.

I wrote a 5-part series of posts on the need for a new service ethos in America. This idea isn’t mine. William F. Buckley wrote about the need in Four Reforms (1974) and in Gratitude (1990). Eric Greitens, Navy SEAL, humanitarian, Rhodes Scholar, charity founder, has promoted service his whole life. I simply echo their great ideas.
