Good morning, Bill!

I wanted to take a moment to introduce myself. My name is Laura and I work with the US Tax Center, a company serving as an educational hub for people with tax questions and providing e-filing services.

It seems like we’ve been talking about the 2016 presidential election for a while now, but there are only a few months left until voters select the nation’s next president. Hillary Clinton and Donald Trump have been sparring on a variety of issues for months, including the topic of taxes.

Both candidates recently proposed tax plans meant to help the middle class, but what they don’t talk about is how those plans might actually impact your paycheck. Luckily, our team has put together an infographic highlighting that information, and the results in one candidate’s words could be “YUGE!”

Let me know if you’d like to take a look at the graphic and I’d love to hear your thoughts!

Have a great day,
Laura Goldstein
