Two cops were ambushed today. One in San Antonio. One in St. Louis. St. Louis Hills. An intersection I know very well from my youth. The St. Louis police sergeant is expected to survive. 

It's time to end the war on cops. Please pray for the wounded officer, his family, our community, and all our fine police officers and emergency responders.
