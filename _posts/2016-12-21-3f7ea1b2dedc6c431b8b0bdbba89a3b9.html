Dear Mr. Hennessy,

I am writing on behalf of RT TV - 24/7 English language news station in Moscow broadcasting internationally.

My name is Ekaterina, I am a producer of the panel discussion program called CrossTalk.

I have the honor to invite you to our program.

We're recording it on Thursday, December 22, at 9 AM St. Louis time

We'll be talking about Sabotaging Trump?

Since his unexpected election the media and liberal political
establish has sought to undermine and de-legitimize Donald Trump.
Should we expect this to continue after he is sworn in January?

Will Trump and the corporate mainstream media ever have a normal relationship?

Agree or disagree: Trump will have a very stormy relationship with the GOP.

Do those who claim Trump is some kind of “Russia agent” also risk
undermining confidence on the electoral process and political
integrity of the country?

How could Trump change the country in a positive way? How could Trump
change the country in a negative way?

It goes without saying that we will be grateful to have you on the program!
Looking forward to hearing from you.

Thank you very much in advance!

Sincerely,
Ekaterina Chekushkina
Producer
CrossTalk
